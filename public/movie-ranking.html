The Best Movies Accordig to Daniel

My top 3 movies of all-time

Made of Honor

Tom is a young man who is afraid of commitments and has several girls. Hannah, his best friend, wants to get married and has now found Mr. Right just as Tom discovers that he really loves Hannah. When she asks to be at her farewell party, Tom takes the opportunity to avoid the wedding and break it up himself.

Hitch: especialista en seduccion

Alex Hitch, love expert, can get even the most mediocre man on a date with the woman of his dreams. Unfortunately, there are also hopeless cases, and that is Albert, who will be a challenge for Hitch.

Friends with benefits

Two lovers decide to find out what happens if they keep emotions away from their physical relationship.
